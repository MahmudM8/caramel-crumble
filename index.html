Founded in 2018, Caramel Crumbs Caf√© began as a small dream in the heart of Abuja. Our founder, a passionate baker and coffee enthusiast, wanted to create a space where people could enjoy artisanal coffee and freshly baked goods in a warm, welcoming atmosphere.
